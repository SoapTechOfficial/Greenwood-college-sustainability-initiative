Why Greenwood?

Sustainability at Greenwood College goes beyond recycling. Through environmental education, school gardens, responsible resource management and student-led initiatives, we are creating a culture of positive environmental change.

School Gardens

Our gardens provide hands-on learning opportunities while promoting biodiversity, food production and environmental awareness. Students actively participate in maintaining green spaces that contribute to a healthier campus.
